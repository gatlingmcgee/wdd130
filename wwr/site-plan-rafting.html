Overview

Purpose

Whitewater rafting will consist of navigating a river in an inflatable raft or kayak downstream through beautiful waters and rapids You can plan on facing many obstacles while attempting to maintain control of the raft with your team of rafters. There are calm scenic floats to adrenaline-fueled runs through drops, waves, and around rocks and boulders.

Audiance

Whitewater rafting can be enjoyed by any one of any age. There are scenic calm routes for those looking to enjoy a day of peace drifting down the river or, there is the nerve shattering rapids with steep drops and many rocks and boulders to navigate around. No matter your age or stomach for the crazy you will enjoy a whitewater adventure with us.
